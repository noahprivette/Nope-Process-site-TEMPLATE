Audience and Goals

Audience

First, I wrote down three sections, Helpers, In need, and Fans. Then I put who I thought would be where, like donors under helpers, homeless under In need, and Homeless under fans. Then I added skeptics to that list, like people who are against helping the police.Lastly I added who I thought would benefit or benefit from the non-profit and who would not benefit from the non-profit.

Overarching Goals

Client

  • Easy ways to reach out to those in need
  • Resources to provide
  • Have the money to provide
  • Get donors
  • Partner with grocery store
  • Contact with other organizations

Audience

  • Find help
  • Contact a staff member or director
  • Donate
  • Get involved

Website

  • Make information easy to get to
  • Inform about programs offered.
  • Get donors
